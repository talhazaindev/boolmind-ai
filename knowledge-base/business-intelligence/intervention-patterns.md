# Intervention Patterns

## approval_bottleneck
Evidence required: approval_process, approval_volume
Interventions (process-first): thresholds, parallel review, workflow automation, AI triage

## cash_conversion_gap
Evidence required: invoicing_timing, collections_process
Interventions: invoicing redesign, collections workflow, payment terms

## crm_pipeline_failure
Evidence required: lead_tracking, handoff_process, current_crm_stack
Interventions: handoff checklist, CRM optimization
