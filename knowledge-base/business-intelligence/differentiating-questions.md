# Differentiating Questions

Ask one question that splits the top two hypotheses:

- **queue_saturation vs exception_handling_gap:** Is delay mostly queue volume waiting for review, or rework from missed exceptions?
- **exception_handling_gap vs ocr_accuracy:** Unusual document types, or standard statements misread?
- **manual_handoff vs capacity_ceiling:** Too many handoffs between teams, or not enough people at the critical step?
- **capacity_ceiling vs data_reentry:** Sheer volume, or re-entering data into multiple systems?

Always reference user's own words or numbers in the question opening.
