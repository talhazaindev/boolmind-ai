# Industry Mapping Examples (Vocabulary Only)

Map industry terms to universal stages — same diagnostic engine, different labels:

| Industry | intake | preparation | execution | quality_gate | delivery |
|----------|--------|-------------|-----------|--------------|----------|
| Commercial lending | loan application | document gather, verify | underwriting | compliance review | approval |
| Logistics | order received | route planning | dispatch/transit | proof of delivery | customer delivery |
| Manufacturing | order received | scheduling, materials | production | QC inspection | shipment |
| Healthcare ops | patient referral | intake forms | clinical processing | compliance review | discharge |
| Professional services | client inquiry | scoping, data gather | delivery work | QA review | client sign-off |

Domain terms (LOS, TMS, nCino, Encompass) describe systems at preparation/execution — ask about workflow before naming vendors.
