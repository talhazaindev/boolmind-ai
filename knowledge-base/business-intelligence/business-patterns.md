# Business Patterns (cross-industry)

Patterns occur across industries — reasoning starts here, not industry labels.

## revenue_up_margin_down
- Signals: revenue growing, margin shrinking, profitability pressure
- Typical capability gaps: collect_revenue, make_decisions

## revenue_up_cash_down
- Signals: revenue up, cash unstable, collections, invoicing
- Typical capability gaps: collect_revenue

## growth_retention_down
- Signals: acquisition up, churn, retention down
- Typical capability gaps: retain_customers, deliver_value
