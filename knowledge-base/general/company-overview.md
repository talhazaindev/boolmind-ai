# Boolmind Company Overview

## Summary

Boolmind builds AI-powered data intelligence products and delivers custom web, mobile, and applied-AI solutions.

## Content

**Catalog products:** **Retify** (retail data unification, 10-step workflow), **ECG Document Intelligence** (clinical ECG extraction, 7-step workflow), **Legal Data Fusion** (legal dataset harmonization, 6-step workflow), and **Forecasting Engine** (ML demand forecasting, 8-step workflow; retail-primary, cross-industry capable).

Each catalog product has distinct workflows — they are not interchangeable. For needs outside these products (e.g. fleet management, bespoke mobile apps, custom ML optimization), Boolmind offers **custom solutions** via web development, mobile apps, applied AI engineering, and AI automation.

Contact sales for pricing; the advisor does not quote prices.
