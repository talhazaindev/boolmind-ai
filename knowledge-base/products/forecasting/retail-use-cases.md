# Forecasting Engine — Retail Use Cases

## Summary

Primary retail scenarios for Forecasting Engine.

## Content

**Demand planning:** Predict weekly SKU sales per store to align purchasing and replenishment.

**Weather-driven inventory:** Adjust stock before heat waves, rain, or seasonal shifts using weather-impact models.

**Promotion planning:** Simulate discount depth and duration; estimate ROI before campaign launch.

**Stockout prevention:** Identify high stockout-risk SKUs; recommend safety stock by location.

**Category strategy:** Hierarchical forecasts reveal category growth, share shifts, and underperforming segments.

**Store benchmarking:** Segment stores by sales patterns; replicate top-performer tactics across the chain.

Retail teams with messy, siloed POS data should consider **Retify first** for unification, then **Forecasting Engine** for forward-looking planning.
