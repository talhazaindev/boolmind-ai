# Forecasting Engine Workflow

## Summary

Forecasting Engine follows an **8-step workflow** from data ingest through actionable insights.

## Content

**Step 1 — Data Ingest:** Connect sales history, inventory, promotions, store metadata, and external signals (weather APIs).

**Step 2 — Feature Engineering:** Build time-series features, seasonality, lag variables, and categorical encodings per product/store.

**Step 3 — Model Training:** Train and validate multiple ML/time-series models; select best performers per hierarchy level.

**Step 4 — Sales Forecasting:** Predict future sales volumes for specific products, stores, and time periods.

**Step 5 — Weather Impact Analysis:** Model how temperature, humidity, precipitation, and wind affect product demand.

**Step 6 — Promotion ROI Analysis:** Evaluate campaign effectiveness, optimize discount strategies, predict promotion lift.

**Step 7 — Inventory & Stockout Risk:** Forecast stockout risk and recommend optimal stock levels to balance revenue and carrying cost.

**Step 8 — Hierarchical Insights & Store Segmentation:** Category-level market share trends, growth patterns, and store clusters with tailored recommendations.

Each step is distinct from Retify (10-step retail unification), ECG (7-step clinical), and Legal (6-step fusion).
