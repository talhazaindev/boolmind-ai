# Forecasting Engine Overview

## Summary

Forecasting Engine is an advanced AI-powered sales forecasting platform that uses machine learning and time series analysis to predict demand, optimize inventory, analyze promotional effectiveness, and deliver real-time business insights.

## Content

Forecasting Engine is **cross-industry capable** with **retail as the primary documented vertical**. It targets operations leaders, demand planners, and data teams who need accurate forward-looking forecasts—not historical data unification (Retify) or document pipelines (ECG/Legal).

Built on FastAPI, PostgreSQL, and multiple ML models. Core capabilities: product/store/period sales forecasting, weather-impact modeling, promotion ROI analysis, stockout risk prediction, hierarchical category forecasting, and store performance segmentation.
