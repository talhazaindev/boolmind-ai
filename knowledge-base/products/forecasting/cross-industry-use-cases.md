# Forecasting Engine — Cross-Industry Use Cases

## Summary

How Forecasting Engine generalizes beyond retail while retail remains the primary reference implementation.

## Content

The same 8-step workflow applies when historical demand or utilization time series exist:

- **CPG / wholesale:** Distributor-level demand by SKU and region.
- **Hospitality:** Occupancy or covers forecasting with weather and event signals.
- **Energy / utilities:** Consumption forecasting with external regressors.
- **Manufacturing:** Finished-goods demand to feed production planning.

Requirements: structured time-indexed data, defined hierarchy (product/location), and measurable outcomes. Verticals without sales/demand time series (e.g. fleet telematics-only, legal contract review) should use **custom solutions**, not Forecasting Engine.

Boolmind can scope cross-industry deployments during a discovery call.
