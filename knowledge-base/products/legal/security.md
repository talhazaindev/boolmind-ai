# Legal Data Fusion Security and Privacy

## Summary

Data privacy and legal compliance for legal dataset processing.

## Content

Legal Data Fusion preserves source traceability and audit logs for every fusion decision. Deployments should enforce role-based access, encryption at rest and in transit, and data residency policies aligned with GDPR and customer legal hold requirements. Sensitive matter data should not leave approved environments when external LLM calls are restricted.
