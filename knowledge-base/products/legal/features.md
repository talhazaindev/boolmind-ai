# Legal Data Fusion Features

## Summary

Core legal data fusion capabilities for consolidated chatbot-ready datasets.

## Content

**Ingestion:** CSV, JSON, XLSX legal and operational datasets.

**Semantic discovery:** LLM-generated dataset and column semantics for heterogeneous sources.

**Clustering:** Automatic grouping of datasets representing the same business entity or matter type.

**Quality diagnosis:** LLM-based issue detection with prioritized remediation lists.

**Human-in-the-loop:** Review UI for mappings, fixes, and recurring rules.

**Golden record:** Harmonized schema output with conflict logs and lineage to source systems.
