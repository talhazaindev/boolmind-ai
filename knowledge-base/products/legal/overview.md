# Legal Data Fusion Overview

## Summary

Legal Data Fusion ingests heterogeneous legal datasets, discovers semantic meaning, clusters related sources, diagnoses quality, and produces golden schema fusion records for chatbot and analytics use cases.

## Content

Legal Data Fusion serves legal operations, compliance officers, and knowledge management teams consolidating contracts, matters, billing, and regulatory datasets. A central orchestrator runs six stages: ingest, discovery, clustering, diagnosis, human-in-the-loop resolution, and golden record fusion with full source traceability.
