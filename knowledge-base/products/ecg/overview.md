# ECG Document Intelligence Overview

## Summary

ECG Document Intelligence automates extraction and standardization of ECG parameters from scanned documents, images, CSV, and waveform formats for EMR-ready clinical data pipelines.

## Content

ECG Document Intelligence targets healthcare IT directors, clinical data managers, and EMR integration teams. The 7-step pipeline handles scanned PDFs, images, CSV, WFDB, and EDF inputs through classification, image preprocessing, multilingual OCR, clinical field extraction, semantic mapping, normalization, and continuous learning.
