# ECG Document Intelligence Features

## Summary

Core capabilities for clinical ECG document processing and EMR integration.

## Content

**Supported inputs:** Scanned PDF ECG reports, JPEG/PNG images, CSV tabular exports, WFDB waveforms, EDF files.

**OCR:** Multilingual clinical OCR with engine fusion for low-quality scans.

**Clinical extraction:** QRS, RR, QT, ST segment, heart rate, rhythm annotations where present in source documents.

**Output:** Structured JSON/HL7-friendly schemas with validation against clinical ranges.

**Integration:** Downstream healthcare analytics, EMR ingestion, research databases.
