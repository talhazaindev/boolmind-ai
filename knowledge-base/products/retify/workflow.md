# Retify — Retail Data Unification Workflow

## Summary

Retify is a 10-step pipeline that ingests multi-format retail data, detects schemas with AI, standardizes and matches entities, diagnoses quality, detects cross-dataset relationships, and exposes NLP-driven insights for chatbot and retail analytics applications.

## Content

### Step 1 — Multi-Format Ingestion

The system accepts retail data from CSV, Excel, PDF, images, JSON, SQL dumps, and ERP logs. Format-specific parsers and OCR convert inputs into structured data.

### Step 2 — AI-Powered Schema Detection

AI analyzes structure and meaning: field meanings, data types, units (currency, weight), and semantic domains. Confidence scores and caching improve accuracy; compliance mode avoids external AI when required.

### Step 3 — Data Standardization

Maps fields to canonical names, normalizes currencies and units, cleans text, and corrects types for predictable structure.

### Step 4 — Entity Matching

Links records representing the same products, customers, stores, or vendors into golden representations.

### Step 5 — Data Quality Diagnosis

Detects missing fields, format issues, anomalies, duplicates, compliance violations, and constraint errors with quality scores.

### Step 6 — Cross-Dataset Relationship Detection

Identifies equivalent fields and referential relationships across datasets for unified analytics.

### Step 7 — Unsupervised Evaluation

Statistical and embedding-based metrics assess schema detection, entity resolution, and relationship confidence.

### Step 8 — Unified Processing Pipeline

Deterministic fixes, statistical corrections, ML anomaly detection, merging, and validation reporting.

### Step 9 — Unified Data Warehouse

Centralized storage with standardized tables, golden master records, and versioning.

### Step 10 — NLP Insights

Natural language to SQL, execution, insights, and visualization suggestions for chatbot queries over unified retail data.
