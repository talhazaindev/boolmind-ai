# Web & Mobile Development

## Summary

Full-stack delivery for custom business applications.

## Content

- **Web:** React/Next.js or similar dashboards, fleet admin consoles, customer portals, REST/GraphQL backends (FastAPI, Node).
- **Mobile:** Native or cross-platform apps for drivers, technicians, store managers.
- **Example:** A fleet management system with vehicle tracking UI, driver workload views, maintenance alerts—built as a **custom Boolmind project**, not a catalog product tour.

Integration with Boolmind catalog products is optional (e.g. Retify-fed warehouse + custom dashboard).
