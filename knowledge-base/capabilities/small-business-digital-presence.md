# Small Business Digital Presence — Phased Boolmind Engagement

## Summary

How Boolmind helps non-technical small businesses establish online presence and enrollment without over-building on day one.

## Content

**Typical SMB profile:** Local service business (academy, studio, clinic, consultancy) with 20–200 customers, mostly word-of-mouth, manual operations (spreadsheets, WhatsApp, bank transfers), limited budget, wants discoverability and online enrollment.

**Boolmind phased approach (3 months):**

1. **Month 1 — Foundation:** Boolmind-scoped landing page showcasing services, testimonials, and contact. Google Business Profile optimization guidance. Professional presence that reflects the brand.
2. **Month 2 — Enrollment:** Online inquiry or enrollment form integrated with their workflow. Scoped to budget — not a full custom platform unless complexity warrants it.
3. **Month 3 — Growth:** SEO basics, analytics review, and scope expansion if enrollment targets are met.

**When to scope custom build:** User needs online payments, scheduling, multi-role access (teachers/parents/admins), or enrollment workflows beyond a simple form.

**Boolmind-first positioning:** Briefly acknowledge DIY tools (Wix, Squarespace) if user raises them, but recommend Boolmind for enrollment integration, professional branding, and growth scalability. Do not quote fixed pricing — offer a discovery call.
