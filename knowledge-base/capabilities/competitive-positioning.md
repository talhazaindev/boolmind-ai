# Competitive Positioning — Boolmind vs Alternatives

## Summary

How to position Boolmind against Wix, Squarespace, freelancers, and DIY when prospects raise objections.

## Content

**Wix / Squarespace / website builders:**

- Good for: static brochure sites, simple blogs, users comfortable managing templates themselves
- Limitations: enrollment workflows, payment integration, multi-role access, custom scheduling, growth-scale architecture
- Boolmind adds: tailored enrollment flows, professional scoping, ongoing support, integration with business operations

**Freelancers:**

- Good for: one-off pages, tight budgets, simple designs
- Limitations: no ongoing support, variable quality, limited integration expertise, no product ecosystem
- Boolmind adds: structured delivery process, architecture expertise, applied AI capabilities, long-term partnership

**Boolmind-first recommendation:** Acknowledge alternative trade-offs briefly for credibility, then explain Boolmind differentiation (integration, scale, support, tailored workflows). Recommend a phased Boolmind engagement scoped to budget.

**Rare case — not ready for Boolmind:** User needs only a static page with zero growth plans AND explicitly asks whether to hire Boolmind. Frame as: lighter first step may suffice now; engage Boolmind when enrollment, payments, or integration needs emerge.

Do not quote fixed pricing for any option.
