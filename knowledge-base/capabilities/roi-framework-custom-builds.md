# ROI Framework for Custom Builds

## Summary

How to evaluate whether a Boolmind custom engagement is worth the investment — without quoting fixed prices.

## Content

**ROI formula:**

```
ROI = (New Revenue + Cost Savings + Retention Value) − Investment
Break-even new customers = Investment ÷ Annual Revenue per Customer
```

**Example (music academy, illustrative):**

- Investment: scoped custom engagement (discuss on discovery call)
- Annual revenue per student: e.g. $1,200/year
- If investment is $5,000: break-even ≈ 4–5 new students in year one
- Additional value: reduced admin time (attendance, fees, parent communication), improved retention, professional credibility

**When Boolmind engagement pays off:**

- Online enrollment replaces manual intake and captures leads 24/7
- Integrated payments reduce fee collection friction
- Professional presence increases discovery beyond word-of-mouth
- Custom workflows (scheduling, parent portals) save staff hours weekly

**Advisor guidance:** Explain the framework first with a worked example, then ask for 1–2 inputs (revenue per customer, current student count). Conclude with when Boolmind adds the most value. Never quote fixed pricing — connect to discovery call.
