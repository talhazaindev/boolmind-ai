# Boolmind Company Capabilities

## Summary

Boolmind is a one-stop technology partner for catalog data products and bespoke engineering.

## Content

Beyond catalog products (Retify, ECG, Legal Data Fusion, Forecasting Engine), Boolmind delivers:

- **Web development:** Full-stack web applications, dashboards, admin portals, APIs.
- **Mobile app development:** iOS/Android and cross-platform apps for field teams, drivers, and operators.
- **Applied AI engineering:** Custom ML models (forecasting extensions, optimization, classification), LLM integrations, computer vision.
- **AI automation solutions:** Workflow automation, intelligent document processing pipelines, RPA with AI layers.

Boolmind can scope, architect, build, and deploy end-to-end solutions when no catalog product is a direct fit.
