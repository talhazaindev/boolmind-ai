# Applied AI Services

## Summary

Custom machine learning and AI engineering offered by Boolmind.

## Content

- **Optimization ML:** Driver workload balancing, route-adjacent planning, resource scheduling (custom scoping required).
- **Forecasting extensions:** Beyond Forecasting Engine catalog—custom hierarchies, proprietary signals.
- **LLM applications:** RAG chatbots, document Q&A, workflow copilots.
- **Computer vision:** Inspection, document capture, field image analysis.
- **MLOps:** Model training pipelines, monitoring, retraining, deployment on cloud or on-prem.

Fleet and transportation ML (e.g. maximizing driver productivity) falls here—not in Retify or Forecasting Engine off-the-shelf.
