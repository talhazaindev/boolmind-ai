# Custom Solutions Engagement Model

## Summary

How Boolmind delivers bespoke solutions versus off-the-shelf catalog products.

## Content

**When to recommend custom solutions:** User needs do not map to Retify, ECG, Legal, or Forecasting Engine—e.g. fleet management, driver workload optimization, transportation logistics apps, **two-sided marketplaces** (fitness trainers, booking platforms), mobile/web startups, payment and scheduling products, industry-specific operational platforms.

**Discovery for bespoke apps:** Gather users, roles, core workflows, payments, notifications, and scale. Payment integration details are scoped during discovery — do not tell users the knowledge base lacks payment docs; Boolmind engineers payment flows as part of custom builds.

**Engagement flow:**
1. Discovery — understand business, pain points, constraints (advisor-led).
2. Scoping — Boolmind architects define components (Boolmind-built vs external).
3. Solution architecture — structured proposal with phases, stack, risks.
4. Build — web/mobile/backend/ML delivery.
5. Deploy & support — production rollout and iteration.

**When to recommend catalog products:** Retail data unification → Retify. Clinical ECG documents → ECG. Legal dataset fusion → Legal. Demand forecasting with sales time series → Forecasting Engine.

The advisor should not quote fixed pricing; offer a discovery call for custom engagements.
